Step 4: J3-J4 Sub-Assembly

  1. Attach J4_A to the J3 motor using 6 M3x6 bolts. Make sure the two Power+CAN ports face the front and rear for easy wiring.
  1. Mount the bearing FL6803ZZ onto J4_B
  1. Place the J4_B on the interior side of J4_A,aligning the holes. Do not fasten them yet.
  1. Carefully slide the J4 motor between J4_A and J4_B. Align it with the holes on J4_B. Refer to the photo for correct positioning. Fasten the motor J4 with part J4_B using 4 M3x6 bolts.
  1. Fasten J4_A into J4_B using 6 M3x6 bolts
  1. Finally, fasten J4_A to the motor J4 using 6 M3x6 bolts.
  1. Carefully insert the post AETTS8-61-SC0-M3-N3 between the flanges of J4_A and fasten them with 2 M3x6 bolts

That concludes the J3-J4 Sub-Assembly
